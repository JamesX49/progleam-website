Partner with ProGleam

We're building a network of established exterior cleaning operators across Ayrshire, Glasgow and central Scotland. Trusted local tradespeople, working under one recognised brand, with shared systems, marketing and support behind them.

A smarter way to grow

The opportunity

Most exterior cleaning businesses are run by one person. You do the cleaning, win the work, handle the marketing and keep the books, and the admin often eats into the time you'd rather spend on the job.

Partnering with ProGleam gives you another option. You carry on doing the work you're good at, with the brand, systems and support we've already put in place behind you. The result is a business that's easier to run, looks more professional, and has room to grow.

What partners gain

What you get from the network

We take on or share the parts that slow most operators down, so you can stay focused on the work.

  • An established, trusted brand to work under
  • A professional website and local marketing working in your area
  • Proven systems for quoting, scheduling and photo-proof customer care
  • Back-office support, so you spend less time on admin and chasing payments
  • The credibility and buying power of being part of a bigger team
Who it's for

Built for established operators

This suits established operators. People who already have their own equipment, transport and insurance, take real pride in their work, and want to grow without losing their independence. If you're based in Ayrshire, Glasgow or nearby and you're good at what you do, we'd like to hear from you.
