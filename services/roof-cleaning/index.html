Roof Cleaning in Kilmarnock & Ayrshire

Moss, algae and dirt removed to restore and protect your roof.

Over time, moss and algae build up on roof tiles, trapping moisture and making a property look tired. ProGleam carefully removes that growth and treats the surface to slow it coming back. That restores the look of your roof and helps protect it through the Ayrshire and Glasgow weather.

We work safely and methodically, clearing debris as we go and leaving the area tidy. You'll get a fixed price up front and before-and-after photos so you can see the difference, even if you're out while we work.

Our focus is roof cleaning and moss removal rather than structural roofing work. We can often arrange minor repairs such as a slipped or cracked tile on request. Just ask when you enquire.

What's included

  • Moss, algae and lichen removed from tiles
  • Gentle softwash treatment to slow regrowth
  • Debris cleared from the roof and gutters
  • Care taken around ridge tiles and flashing
  • Tidy-up and photo proof on completion
  • Fully insured, safe working at height
