Cookie Policy

How this website uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may also be used to load fonts, maps, forms or other website features.

2. Essential website use

Our website is a static website and does not currently use a customer login, shopping basket or account area. Some basic technical processing may still happen when your browser loads pages, images, fonts, maps or forms.

3. Third-party services

This website loads Google Fonts to display the site typography. Some pages, including the homepage and the Contact page, embed an OpenStreetMap map to show the areas we cover. Quote and contact forms are submitted through Web3Forms so your enquiry can be sent to us. These services may receive technical information such as your IP address, browser details and the page you are using.

4. Analytics cookies and your consent

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site, such as which pages are viewed and how people reach us, so we can improve the website. When enabled, Google Analytics may set cookies and collect information such as a randomised identifier, your approximate location, and your device, browser and page activity.

We use Google Consent Mode. When you first arrive, analytics is set to denied by default, so no analytics cookies are used until you decide. A banner asks you to Accept or Decline. If you Accept, analytics cookies are turned on and your choice is remembered on your device. If you Decline, analytics stays off. You can change your choice at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser, which brings the banner back.

We do not use advertising or remarketing cookies, and we do not sell your personal data.

5. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies or third-party requests may affect how fonts, maps or forms appear or work.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies or this policy? Contact ProGleam Ltd at info@progleam.co.uk.
